Contracts and termination

The termination conditions are specific to each contract, given the range of products and services offered.

You may send an electronic termination request to the following e-mail address: mathilde.pebre@icloud.com . You will receive a notification confirming that your request has been received.

Your request must include the following information:

– Full name if the contract was concluded with an individual, or company name if it was concluded with a legal entity.
– E-mail address or, if not available, a postal address to receive confirmation of receipt of the termination notice.
– Any reference allowing the identification of your contract and its holder.
– The desired termination date, subject to applicable legal or contractual provisions.

If the requested termination is early or does not comply with the conditions stipulated in the contract, you must provide a legitimate reason, and this may lead to a discussion as provided for in the contract terms.
