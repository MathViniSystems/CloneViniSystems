Softwares and automations

ViniSystems is also a set of customised computer solutions designed to simplify your data management and domain-specific reasoning. I created them based on my own experiences, thinking of what would have made my work easier and saved time during my missions.
ViniSystems Management App
ViniSystems App is an application available on computer and phone, combining a set of tools added on request to suit your needs. Its purpose is to centralise data creation and management, avoiding human errors and loss of information, with a welcoming and easy-to-use interface.

Forget about:
- Searching for how much tartaric acid you added during harvest across every analysis report or Excel file, which might not even have been written down;
- Last-minute tastings that make it hard to focus on relevant attributes and end up losing valuable information for the next vintage;
- Urgently hunting for the bottler or filter manual lost somewhere, only to find the instructions are never clear enough to solve the problem easily;
- “Did I sulphite this barrel?” “How much did I put?” “When was it?”;
- Product doses calculated quickly without leaving any trace.

ViniSystem Traceability
ViniSystems Traceability is a traceability software available on computer, including only the features you need, ensuring easy handling while allowing you to follow your wines and meet regulatory requirements. A solution designed for small wineries, providing a complete and customisable tool that is easier to use than larger systems mainly intended for big estates.

ViniSystems Traceability is still under development; a first version will be available soon.

Forget about:
- Multiple Excel files for traceability tracking that make information difficult to find;
- Information scattered everywhere: analyses, transfers, product additions, fermentation monitoring… everything is centralised.


But that’s the aim of any traceability software; what I really want is to:
- Simplify a tool that is often far too complex for the needs of small wineries;
- Integrate management tools like my predictive model for fermentation issues;
- Include as much automation as possible so data entry is as quick as it can be.
