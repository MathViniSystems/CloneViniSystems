Mathilde Artificial Intelligence

Mathilde AI is my personalised virtual assistant. I designed it in my image, with my own body of knowledge, in order to replicate my working method.

Throughout my studies and professional journey, I reformulated all the knowledge I acquired in class, in books and in scientific articles, and I deepened it to build a very complete and detailed set of resource documents covering the whole of viticulture and oenology. I wanted to create my personal database, to keep updating it as new discoveries arise so that I never lose knowledge over time. I gathered all these documents to duplicate my knowledge and my method in an autonomous model capable of:
– answering questions by drawing on my own knowledge.
– responding to any request instantly, adapting to your working hours and saving you time.
– always staying up to date with the evolution of scientific knowledge thanks to its ability to self-learn from the latest published research.
– mastering critical thinking and being able to answer only with knowledge whose accuracy I personally guarantee.

Mathilde AI is still in development to ensure the most knowledgeable and high-performing model possible; it will be available soon.
